Sample Feedback

Anonymized examples of professional UX findings

Navigation & Information Architecture

Hidden Primary CTA on Mobile

Issue

The primary "Get Started" button is positioned below the fold on mobile devices (viewport height < 700px). Users must scroll past a hero image and two paragraphs of text before encountering the main conversion action.

Impact

60% of mobile visitors may not see the CTA without scrolling. This creates unnecessary friction in the conversion funnel and may result in lost signups, particularly for users who make quick decisions.

Recommendation

Reposition the CTA within the initial viewport on mobile (above 600px). Consider a sticky header CTA or reducing hero content height. A/B test shows ~23% lift in mobile conversions when primary action is immediately visible.

Accessibility & Inclusivity

Insufficient Color Contrast in Form Validation

Issue

Error messages displayed in light red (#FF6B6B) on white background fail WCAG AA standards for color contrast (2.8:1 ratio vs. required 4.5:1). Users with low vision or color blindness may not perceive validation errors.

Impact

Users may repeatedly submit forms with errors without understanding why submission fails. This particularly affects the 8% of male users with red-green color deficiency and users with situational limitations (bright sunlight, aging displays).

Recommendation

Darken error text to #C92A2A (7.0:1 contrast). Add error icons alongside color. Include aria-live announcements for screen readers. Consider inline validation to catch errors before submission.

Performance & Technical UX

Blocking Script Impact on Interactive Timing

Issue

Third-party analytics script (425KB uncompressed) loads synchronously in document head, blocking page render. Time to Interactive measures 4.8s on 3G connections. Users see blank screen during initial load.

Impact

53% of mobile users abandon pages that take longer than 3 seconds to load. The blocking script creates a perceived performance issue even though content is ready. Poor Core Web Vitals may also impact search rankings.

Recommendation

Move script to async/defer or load post-render. Consider removing analytics from critical path entirely. Lazy-load non-essential third-party tools. Target TTI under 2.5s for optimal mobile experience.
